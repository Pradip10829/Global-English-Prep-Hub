IELTS

Balanced test for study, work, and migration goals

Band 0-9

Best for students who want a familiar four-skill exam with a live speaking interview and globally recognized band scoring.

  • Great fit for UK, Canada, Australia, and other broad-use pathways.
  • Clear skill-by-skill feedback helps identify where scores can rise fastest.
  • Well suited to students who perform better in human conversation.

PTE

Fast digital exam with integrated task scoring

Score 10-90

Ideal for students who want a fully computer-based experience, repeatable templates, and a compact testing workflow.

  • Strong option when fast results and digital comfort matter.
  • Integrated tasks reward efficient pacing and repeat-practice systems.
  • Useful for students who prefer structured, keyboard-first preparation.

TOEFL

Academic English proof for university admissions

Score 0-120

Designed for students targeting institutions that value lecture comprehension, academic writing, and integrated speaking performance.

  • Best when your goals are academic and source-based communication matters.
  • Section scoring helps students focus on academic listening and synthesis gaps.
  • Strong fit for university-focused English preparation.
